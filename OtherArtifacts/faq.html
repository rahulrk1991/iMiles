Frequently Asked Questions | FAQs

Overview

  1. What is interviewmile?

    Interviewmile is a platform which helps you to prepare for interviews, be interview-ready and even take an employability test and get interview calls according to your caliber without being judged in a crowd. It contains comprehensive study material which you can prepare from.

  2. How to do I see my profile?

    Click on your name which appears on top right to go to your profile, alternatively click on the menu bottom on the rightmost corner and choose the first option profile.

  3. What is QnA Crunch?

    QnA crunch contains miscellaneous questions answered by professionals. The topic wise practice questions, which are cross referenced amongst each other, are super easy to understand. On the top right corner of your window you can see the menu button. On clicking, a drop down menu appears. The second option is QnA crunch. Browse for questions and click on ‘Solv’ to see the explained solution of the problem. You can also add a question to your favorites’ by clicking on the heart below the question to find it easily later. Select any category from the category list, given on the right pane, to see questions related to that topic.

  4. What is Favorite Questions List?

    The third option you can see in the menu is Favorite Questions List. Any question that you marked in your QnA Crunch section by clicking on the heart below it, will appear here. Like these you can sort out all the questions that you feel are really important or interesting.

  5. What is Puzzling Puzzles?

    The Puzzling Puzzles contains mind boggling logical questions that steer up your thinking capability and help you think on a whole new level. On the top right corner of your window you can see the menu button. On clicking, a drop down menu appears. The fifth option is Puzzling Puzzles. Try the problems on your own or seek help by clicking on ‘View Solution’.

  6. What are online mock tests?

    The third option in the menu is the Online mock test are practice test sets from a wide range of topics, after taking the test your performance is analyzed and presented to you in the form of score and experience points. These test are won’t cost you a dime, but are great for practicing. Just click on ‘Start Test’. Answer the questions by clicking on the choice you feel is correct. When done with the entire questions click on ‘Submit Test’. Your score and remaining time will be shown on the screen, and the correct answers along with the explanation will be displayed. You can see your progress via your score and experience points.

  7. What are score and experience points?

    The lightning bolt on the top right corner shows your score is the total marks you obtained in all the tests you have taken, while the graduations cap next to shows your experience points show the number of questions you have solved.

  8. How can I be “interview-ready”?

    When you prepare from interviewmile you get a refined study material with proper direction, rather than flipping pages of a number of books and still not know what to prepare. So rather than being a hard worker you can be a smart worker and get your dream job faster.

  9. How do I prepare?

    Interviewmile is a hotspot of exercises and questions, which you can solve and analyze yourself on the basis of your performance. Choose any of our mock tests, go for some puzzles or crunch on some QnA.

  10. How can I see my progress?

    You can see your progress in the form of score and experience points. Also you can see a statistical analysis on your profile, which is the direct representation of the questions you have solved from different topics.

  11. How do I change my email address?

    Changing you email address is not an issue, all you need to do is: Go to the menu. Click on account settings. Edit whatever you want to Click on Update button. Or to change is altogether click on Reset.
